About Me

My full name is Chisom Daisy Nwobodo. I am a 19 year old undergraduate, currently studying Industrial Mathemathics-Computer Science at Covenant University. With a diverse background in design and technology, I specialize in crafting digital experiences that are both functional and visually compelling. I am currently a fellow at Headstarter, where I am honing my skills in software engineering to complement my design expertise. I also love art and all forms of it!

MY DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
Beliefs:
I believe that great design stems from a deep understanding of user needs and a commitment to creating solutions that are both intuitive and impactful. My design philosophy centers around empathy, simplicity, and inclusivity, ensuring that every user feels valued and understood.

Process:
My approach to design begins with comprehensive research and user engagement. I prioritize gathering insights through user interviews, surveys, and usability testing. This research forms the foundation for my ideation and prototyping phases, where I focus on creating user-friendly, aesthetically pleasing designs. Iteration is key; I continually refine my work based on feedback, ensuring that the final product meets the highest standards of usability and design excellence.

Unique Perspective:
My background in both UI/UX design and software engineering allows me to seamlessly integrate functionality with visual appeal. I draw inspiration from modern art, nature, and technological advancements, which influences my clean, innovative design style. My multicultural experiences provide a diverse lens through which I approach design challenges, allowing me to create solutions that resonate with a wide audience.

Goals and Impact:
I strive to design products that not only fulfill business objectives but also contribute to the greater good. Accessibility is a core tenet of my work; I aim to create inclusive designs that cater to users of all abilities. Through thoughtful, user-centric design, I seek to enhance everyday experiences and make a meaningful impact on society.
